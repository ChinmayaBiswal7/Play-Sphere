🎾 MATCH FLOW

  • Match Formats: Select from Quick Tennis, One Set, or Best of 3 Sets on the match setup screen.
  • Game Scoring: Points score as LOVE → 15 → 30 → 40 → GAME. Tie-breaks are played at 6-6 games.
  • Change of Ends: Players switch court halves after the first game and every odd-numbered game in a set.

⚡ SERVING LAWS

  • Serve Side: Serves alternate court sides every point. Start on the Right (Deuce) side, then Left (Advantage).
  • Diagonal Target: Ball must land in the diagonally opposite service box. The target box is highlighted in your viewport.
  • Faults & Lets: First serve out is a Fault. Second out is a Double Fault. Net touch that lands in is a Let (replay serve).

🥎 BALL IN PLAY

  • Volleys: Volleys are legal during rallies, but prohibited on the immediate serve return (ball must bounce once first).
  • Net Collisions: Net touches during rallies are in play if the ball crosses. Fails to cross awards point to opponent.
  • Double Bounce: Hitting the ball after a second floor bounce awards the point to your opponent.

🎮 CONTROLLER & KEYBOARD

  • Keyboard (P1): WASD/Arrows to Move; Space / J for MID; K for LONG; L for SMASH; I to DIVE.
  • Virtual Stick: Responsive steering across court.
  • MID / LONG: Safe mid-court return vs deep baseline targeting.
  • SMASH: Heavy downward spike shot when ball is high.
  • DIVE: Execute athletic saving dives when moving.
